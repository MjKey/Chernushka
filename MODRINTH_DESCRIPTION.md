## ‚ú¶ About

Chernushka is a tameable creature that follows you around, helps you break blocks, and can even merge with others to become a giant fluffy helper.

---

## ‚ú¶ Features

### Rare Spawn
Chernushkas spawn rarely in any biome. Keep your eyes open while exploring ‚Äî finding one is a lucky encounter!

### Tameable Companion
Simply right-click a wild Chernushka with an empty hand to befriend it. Once tamed, it will follow you everywhere ‚Äî through caves, across oceans, and into the Nether.

### Mining Assistant
Your Chernushka watches what you're doing. When you start breaking a block, nearby Chernushkas will gather around and speed up the process. The more helpers, the faster you mine.

Use the **Chernushka Stick** to assign specific blocks for your companions to mine on their own.

### Merge System
Feed two Chernushkas with **Coal** or **Flint** to merge them into a bigger one. Keep merging to create an absolute unit ‚Äî up to 5x the original size! Use a **Feather** to split them back apart.

### Smooth Animations
Powered by GeckoLib, Chernushkas have fluid walk, run, jump, mining, and idle animations. Their eyes even follow you around.

### Survival-Friendly
- Immune to drowning, fall damage, and suffocation
- Walks on the ocean floor instead of swimming
- Teleports to you when too far away
- Persists through world reloads

## ‚ú¶ Interactions

| Action | Result |

## ‚ú¶ Configuration

All settings can be adjusted in-game via **Mod Menu** (requires Cloth Config), or by editing `config/chernushka.json`:

- Mining help toggle and speed bonus
- Follow distance and teleport threshold  
- Walk/run speeds
- Mining message notifications
